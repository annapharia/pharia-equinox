The science

Targeted Brain Stimulation

Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS) uses precisely targeted magnetic pulses to optimize brain activity in neural networks tied to focus, presence, and emotional reactivity. Adapted from 15 years of clinical use for higher-functioning individuals who want more control over how they think, feel, and perform.

One-Day Protocol, Rapid Results

Skip the weekly appointments. Our accelerated protocol delivers a full treatment course in a single day — with most people feeling shifts within 24 hours.

Non-Invasive

No surgery, no downtime. TMS works through magnetic fields that pass safely through the skull to target specific brain regions with precision.

FDA-Cleared Since 2008

First cleared by the FDA in 2008, TMS has over 15 years of safe clinical use. Pharia has adapted it specifically for high-performers to dial in their mental performance.
