The science behind it

Transcranial magnetic stimulation (TMS) uses precisely calibrated magnetic pulses to modulate neural circuit activity. It's the same technology used in clinical settings for over 15 years—now applied with a personalized, accelerated protocol.

This is clinic-grade equipment administered by trained specialists. Non-invasive. No medication. No systemic side effects. Just targeted optimization at the neurological level.
