The Science

Targeted Brain Stimulation

Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS) uses precisely targeted magnetic pulses to optimize brain activity in neural networks tied to focus, presence, and emotional reactivity.

Non-Invasive

No surgery, no downtime. TMS works through magnetic fields that pass safely through the skull to target specific brain regions with precision.

Safe & Well-Tolerated

First cleared by the FDA in 2008, TMS has over 15 years of safe clinical use. TMS is well-tolerated. Most people don't have side effects. If they do, it's mild and improves quickly. Risk of adverse events is extremely rare. (Cleveland Clinic)
